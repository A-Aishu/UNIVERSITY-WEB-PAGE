The School of Computing, Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology welcomes you all. The School of Computing comprises of Department of Computer Science and Engineering and Department of Information Technology. The Department of Computer Science and Engineering provides B.E. Computer Science and Engineering with NBA accreditation, B.E. Computer Science and Engineering with specialization in Artificial Intelligence, B.E. Computer Science and Engineering with specialization in Data Sciences, B.E. Computer Science and Engineering with specialization in IoT, M.E. Computer Science and Engineering programmes. The Department of Information Technology provides B.Tech Information Technology. The school also offers Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering, B.Sc. Computer Science, M.Sc. Computer Science and many certification courses. The School has MoU with top-notch industries like IBM, Virtusa, Oracle, VMWare, RedHat, Cisco and InfoSys to share knowledge and experience to our students. The school also has Centre of Excellence with CapGemini in order to bridge the gap between industry and academia. Our placements are consistently above 92% for the past three years. Our students are placed with highest salary of 41 lakhs per annum and an average salary of 4 lakhs per annum. Students are guided for placements right from the first year. The students and faculty of the school of computing to their credit have 30 patents, 4 copyrights, 10 in-house projects developed for the Institution and have completed sponsored projects worth 2 crores so far. We cater to various needs of our students. Students are encouraged to be part of various Student Development Clubs to horn skills of their passion. Our students have built onboard-computing device for the Sathyabama SAT, our flagship in Indian History. Our students were selected as ambassadors for Google, NASA, IBM and have represented our Institution in various stages. The student-abroad programmes have enabled our students to acquire internships, semester-abroad studies in various international universities across the globe.
